By His grace and for His glory.

Stories of Light, Truth, and God's Faithfulness

DANIEL GOODWIN

I am a follower of Jesus, a husband, and a storyteller with a passion for truth. After over two decades in the U.S. Air Force, I’ve turned my heart toward writing books that reflect God’s light, the power of Scripture, and the beauty of redemption.

My writing blends timeless storytelling with biblical truth, aimed especially at young Christian men and families. Whether through adventure, parable, or children’s tales, my goal is to point people to Jesus and inspire a life of faith.

Thank you for visiting. May your heart be stirred toward hope and courage in Christ.
